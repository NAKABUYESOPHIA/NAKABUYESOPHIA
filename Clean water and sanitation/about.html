Our history

The Clean Water and Sanitation project founded Nakabuye Sophia in 2023 is a community driven initiative aimed at addressing water scarcity and improving sanitation in underprivileged

Nakabuye as an environmental activist, recognized the critical need for clean and accessible water and proper sanitation facilities, especially in rural areas

The project focuses on improving safe drinking water through the construction wells and boreholes as well as educating the community about hygiene practices to prevent waterborne diseases

Additionally, it works on creating sustainable sanitation such as promoting water management practices to enhance overall health and wellbeing

Our Mission

To ensure that clean water ,safe drinking water and good sanitation facilities are accessed by every person

Our Vission

A world where every person has access to clean water and lives in communities with sustainable sanitation solutions
